Fabi Token creation documents

That's why; FABİ Token will be able to replace cash, meal vouchers and tickets, which were once used as a means of payment in the food and beverage industry in the developing digital world.
FABI Token; it is not just built to meet individual demands. FABI Token will bring convenience to the Food and Beverage Industry for all corporate companies and businesses operating in different sectors all over the world.
